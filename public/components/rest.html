Jewelry

Keep it delicate and minimal with gold jewelry. Think dainty necklaces, simple hoops, or understated studs. Clear gemstones are welcome for a touch of sparkle, but please avoid pearls.

Hair & Makeup

Hair and makeup will be your responsibility. Go for soft, romantic waves—either flowing down or elegantly half-up with delicate twists or braids. Keep makeup fresh and natural with a glowing complexion and nude or pink lipstick. Think effortlessly beautiful!

Nails

Choose a classic look with natural nude tones, elegant French tips, or soft pink shades. Feel free to pick any nail shape and length that makes you comfortable and confident—whether that's short and sweet or long and elegant.
